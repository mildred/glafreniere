GENESIS

The electron creation from aether plane waves.

Assuming that an aether filled with waves exists, our universe must exist.

Without any existing electron, this situation is not likely to happen, but it must happen. One chance out of billions. 

With billions of existing electrons, which wavelength is almost the same, this situation is much more probable.

 

In the beginning of times, some immaterial Entity created an aether filled with waves.

 By the seventh second the Entity had completed all His work and He rested.

Genesis.

 

Then the continuation was foreseeable:

  1. The waves created a lot of electrons and positrons.

  2. Electrons and positrons were attracted together and created a lot of up and down quarks.

  3. Some up and down quarks joined together and generated gluonic fields, protons and neutrons.

  4. Protons attracted electrons and produced hydrogen atoms.

  5. Hydrogen atoms were attracted by gravity and produced stars.

  6. Stars became supernovae and produced more complicated atoms.

  7. All these atoms produced a 2nd generation of stars and planets, the Sun and the Earth.

  8. Atoms became more complex arrays of molecules.

  9. Complex molecules on Earth became living cells capable of duplicating themselves.

  10. Some cells joined together in the sea and generated more complex organisms.

  11. Those organisms had to adapt themselves and evolved in the sea according to Darwin.

  12. Evolved organisms became animals living at the expense of plants or other animals.

  13. Some animals became fishes, which are aquatic vertebrates.

  14. Some of the fishes used their fins in order to survive out of the water and became reptilians.

  15. Some of the reptilians became mammals.

  16. Some of the mammals became primates.

  17. Some of the mammal primates started thinking and they became homo sapiens.
